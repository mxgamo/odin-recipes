Delicious Chicken 65 Recipe

Chicken 65 is a spicy, deep-fried chicken dish originating from Hotel Buhari, Chennai, India. It is personally one of my favorite dishes because of its spice and richness of flavor! Thats why it's the first recipe on my website, I want you to experince the goodness asap :)

Ingredients you'll need:
  • Chicken
  • Dehi
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Spices
  • Curry Leaves
  • Flours
